Hire-Pro User Guide

Introduction

Welcome to Hire-Pro, your comprehensive ATS solution. This guide will help you understand how to use the software effectively for managing candidates, job postings, and generating reports.

Logging In

Step 1: Access the Login Page

Navigate to the Hire-Pro login page at http://yourdomain.com/login.

Step 2: Enter Credentials

Enter your username and password, then click the "Login" button.

Dashboard Overview

Once logged in, you will be directed to the dashboard. The dashboard provides a quick overview of the key metrics and recent activities.

Managing Candidates

Step 1: View Candidates

Click on "Candidates" in the sidebar to view all the candidates. You can search and filter candidates based on different criteria.

Step 2: Add a New Candidate

Click on the "Add Candidate" button. Fill in the candidate details and click "Save" to add the candidate to the database.

Step 3: Edit Candidate Details

To edit a candidate's details, click on the candidate's name from the list, make the necessary changes, and click "Save".

Managing Job Postings

Step 1: View Job Postings

Click on "Job Postings" in the sidebar to view all job postings. You can search and filter job postings based on different criteria.

Step 2: Add a New Job Posting

Click on the "Add Job" button. Fill in the job details and click "Save" to add the job posting to the database.

Step 3: Edit Job Posting Details

To edit a job posting, click on the job title from the list, make the necessary changes, and click "Save".

Generating Reports

Step 1: Access Reports

Click on "Reports" in the sidebar to access the reporting module.

Step 2: Generate a Report

Select the report criteria and click "Generate". You can download the report in Excel format by clicking the "Download" button.
